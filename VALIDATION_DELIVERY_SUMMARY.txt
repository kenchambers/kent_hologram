================================================================================
CRITICAL DESIGN DECISIONS MADE
================================================================================

1. RESIDUE SUBTRACTION (CRITICAL - Section A1 of Addendum)
   Problem: Direct subtraction violates HDC properties
   Solution: Use inverse bundling instead
   Code: residue = bundle(residue, -positioned_recon)

2. HIERARCHICAL GATING MECHANISM (Section C of Addendum)
   Problem: Additive augmentation breaks normalization
   Solution: Use multiplicative gating via binding
   Code: bind(relation_vec, gate_vec) then bundle with obs

3. SOLVER STRATEGY SELECTION (Section 4 of Executive Summary)
   Problem: Fixed strategy at init is inflexible
   Solution: Implement HYBRID at call time with fallback
   Pattern: Try single-step → multi-step → iterative

4. RELATION EXTRACTION CAP (Section B2 of Addendum)
   Problem: More than 30 relations detected
   Solution: Sort by confidence, select top-30
   Code: relations_sorted[:30]
